============== Adding a new completeness detection ======================

Add a new completeness detection class by implementing the interface
at ch.unibas.informatik.hs15.cs203.datarepository.api.CompletenessDetection

Add your new implementation by putting them in data-repository-plugin.jar

and adding the following to your ant script:

<manifest>
<attribute name="Main-Class" value="..."/>
<attribute name="Class-Path"
value="data-repository.jar data-repository-plugin.jar"/>
</manifest>
